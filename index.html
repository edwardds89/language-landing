Questions & answers

What is LanguagePin?
LanguagePin is a language learning app that ties vocabulary to the real places where you'll actually use it — cafes, markets, gyms, transport. Instead of abstract flashcards, you learn the words and phrases you need for a specific place, so when you get there you can actually speak.
How does it work?
You pick a place on the map — say, a coffee shop in Soho — and LanguagePin gives you the vocabulary, phrases and pronunciation you need for that exact context. You practice before you go, then use it for real. The app also uses AI to adapt to your level and the language you're learning.
Which languages are supported?
At launch LanguagePin supports English, Italian, French, Spanish, German and Portuguese. More languages are on the roadmap based on what founding members need most.
Is LanguagePin available outside London?
The map launches in London first, but founding members get full AI access from day one regardless of where they are. The founding price is locked in wherever you sign up, and new cities will roll out after London.
What is a Founding Member?
Founding Members are the first 500 people to join the LanguagePin waitlist. They get early access before the public launch, the founding price locked in for life, and a direct line to shape the product. Once the 500 spots are gone, this offer closes.
How many founding spots are there?
Exactly 500. This is a hard cap — not a marketing number. We're keeping the founding cohort small so we can give each member real attention and build the product together.
When does LanguagePin launch?
LanguagePin is currently in private beta with a small group of founding learners. Founding members on the waitlist will be the first to get access when we open. Sign up to secure your spot and get launch updates — no spam, ever.
Is LanguagePin free?
Trying LanguagePin is free: you get free AI practice sessions and the map is open to everyone. Premium unlocks the full venue experience — on the free plan, venue discovery is supported by a small selection of sponsored spots. Founding members lock the founding price in for life, significantly better than the standard price at public launch. Joining the waitlist is free and there's no commitment until we launch.
